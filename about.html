Jason Sammons, DMD

S. Jason Sammons, D.M.D., board certified by the American Board of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, has been in practice with Oral Surgery Associates of West Tennessee since 2001. He is a member of the surgical staff at Regional Hospital of Jackson. He earned his doctoral degree from the University of Louisville with honors and completed his presidency in oral and maxillofacial surgery at the University of Texas Medical Branch in Galveston.

Dr. Sammons' interests in oral and maxillofacial surgery include preprosthetic surgery, dental implants and oral and maxillofacial pathology. Dr. Sammons is a fellow of the American Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons.
